Info

  • Brother Print SDK handles QL-8xxc as QL-8xx and QL-11xxc as QL-11xx.
  • An android developer can be use QL-8xxc and QL-11xxc with no update.
  • An iOS developer, who supports QL-8xxc or QL-11xxc over Network, can be use these models with no update.
  • An iOS developer, who supports QL-8xxc or QL-11xxc over Bluetooth, needs to update to 4.6.1 and require MFi approval when publishing in App store.
